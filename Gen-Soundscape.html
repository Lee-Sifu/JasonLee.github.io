Process & Intentions: Another work made in MaxMSP 8, I made a generative soundscape system. I did this to experiment rather than doing the traditional way of music/sound making. The reference page was useful when learning each block. I experimented by testing the connected blocks and got many different generative sounds while adding some interactive functions. I wanted to give people a whole new experience with a generative sound based on different parameters they could play with.
