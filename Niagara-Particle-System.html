Medium(s): UE5, MaxMSP 8, Photography, FL Studio

Credits: Jack Kim (Watch Video)

Process & Intentions: In Unreal Engine 5.4, I utilized Unreal’s Niagara system to make an immersive particle system. I was inspired by another artist who used Niagara to make an immersive particle and I added my own changes to it by adding some interactive functions such as immersive generated soundscape from MaxMSP and edited in Fl. I thought of using photos as textures instead of creating a custom material for a more dynamic look. I hoped to provide people with a good experience by playing around with the simulated effects while feeling immersed. Experimenting with Unreal’s powerful Niagara system helped me think of how I can use it in other ways besides in games. I hope to further explore it and come up with new ways to create in Unreal and maybe merge with other mediums.
